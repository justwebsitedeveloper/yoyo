What Is YOYO?

The first ever Mobile App to bring about an unimaginable paradigm shift in your relationship with your favourite celebrities

YOYO

YOYO is the long awaited, socially-engaging App that bridges the distance between you and your favourite STARZ in real time, in the real world.

Since the very beginning of time, you have seen the existence of a major divide—that of the world of celebrities, aka STARZ, and the world of fans. The world of celebrities has existed and flourished on the love, respect and admiration of the fans. Yet, the two worlds have never really met.

Millions of fans live their lives for that one chance to see their favourite STARZ, talk to them, shake hands with them, take a selfie or get an autograph. Only very few manage to get that close to celebrities. The rest live in hope, hope that either gets fulfilled or gets crushed. And the noise of failing hopes can be quite deafening.

Hence was found the YOYO App. YOYO is that gateway that you have been looking for all your life to reach your favourite STARZ. With YOYO, STARZ are never again going to be far away from you. YOYO brings your chosen celebrities to your very doorstep, at the touch of your very fingers.
